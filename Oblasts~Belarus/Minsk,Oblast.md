## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> Minsk region, also known as Minsk oblast or Minsk voblasts, is one of the six regions of Belarus. 
> Its administrative center is Minsk, 
> although that is a separate administrative territorial entity of Belarus. 
> The region's population was recorded at 1,411,500 in 2011.
>
> [Wikipedia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Minsk%20region)
